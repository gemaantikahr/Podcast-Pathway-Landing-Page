How it works

Two simple paths to a booking

Whether you run a show or want to appear on one, you're a few steps from your next collaboration.

If you're a host
1
Create your podcast profile
Show guests what your show is about and who listens.
2
Post an opportunity or search the directory
Describe the guest you need, or go find them directly.
3
Review, select, and invite
Compare applicants by profile and match score.
4
Message and schedule the recording
Agree the details and get it on the calendar.
If you're a guest
1
Build your professional profile
Your expertise, experience, and topics — once.
2
Browse opportunities that match
Filter by category, format, and audience.
3
Apply, or accept invitations
Hosts can also find and invite you directly.
4
Confirm and record
Chat with the host and lock in the session.
